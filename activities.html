Activities

Help the environment! If you have trash don't throw it away, instead create something new with it. A one-of-a-kind item.
So here are somethings you can do:

Plant a few trees!

Trees! Trees! Trees! Trees help keep our air clean and will help replace all the trees we have cut down and help our planet.

Go get your hands dirty and go plant some trees!

Make some jewelry! For you or someone else.

Make a cute necklace from a bottle cap and earrings made from an old soda can!

What about a ring made from a bottle cap, some sprinkles, and glue!

Ok now let me show you some more items, you can create from items you may think you can't do anything with:

Help the birds! Make a birdfeeder out of a plastic bottle, or make one out of a milk carton!

Got some old CD's that you don't know what to do with? Well here are a few items you can make:

Make some room decor! Decorate your wall and make a low hanging canopy of CD's.

Decorate a plate made of cut up CD's. Make a disco ball, and throw the best party ever!

How about some items to organize your home? Well here are a few to please you!

Millions of plastic bottles end up in landfills every year. A good way to make use of it is to make something! Pen holders, plant holders, candy holders, whatever suits you.

Piggy banks make excellent gifts for younger siblings. They are fun and easy to make!

Using garbage to make a gift to someone is not only creative, but helps the environment too! Create a vase out of a plstic bottle. Or create some holders in the shapes of apples, and put little gifts in them. Now this would be a better gift holder then some boring old party bags.

Paper rolls could be turned into a one-of-a-kind phone holder! Shampoo bottles make great two in one phone holders and chargers!

Now it's game time!
